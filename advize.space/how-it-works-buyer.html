How it works ?

Get any work done in just a few steps ahead

Advize provides a platform for all users to get any type of work done. Buyer can make his work done by conducting screening to sellers or give a open challenge. Buyer can upload anytype of projects and find suitable seller.

Timeline of Buyer's profile

Individual / Team Project

Enter Advize
Enter project details and submit.
Notifications are sent to sellers about the project.

Connect with Sellers / Investors

Sellers apply to the project submitted by Buyer or Buyer finds a Seller with experience in various projects.
Buyer and Investor interact with each other discussing the project's motive.
Screening process is done to the list of interested people.
Selected candidates after screening will be in connection with Buyer.
Project will be aborted if no suitable candidates are found within the restricted time.

Confirmation & Milestones

The total project can be divided into parts and be given to different people to work.
Buyer sets the timeline and milestones to the team or individual.
Once the milestones and timeline are agreed, Buyer makes payment to Advize.
Seller initiates work.
The Project begins and the search ends.

Open Project

Enter Advize
Enter project details and submit.
Notifications are sent to all users about the project.
Sellers are allowed to register for the notified Project.
Buyer makes payment to Advize once the registrations are closed.
Sellers initiate work.

Confirmation & Milestones

If the total project is divided into tasks, Seller can work on any of the tasks.
Buyer divides the payment for each set of tasks.
Buyer selects a seller in each task as qualified to be paid.
The project completion is updated by buyer and sellers get paid.
The Project begins and the search ends.
