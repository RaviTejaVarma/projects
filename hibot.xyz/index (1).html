What we Do ?

MMPC is bringing Machine Iintelligence and its applications into our daily life. With the support of our researchers and enthusiasts we are working on building
Aryabhata or Aryabhata-I new gen AI platform.

  • Aryabhata - I

    New Gen AI platform

    Our New Generation Artificial Intelligence platform is named after famous Indian Mathematician and Astronomer Aryabhata. Inspired by machine learning and related technologies we are focused on building our platform utilising AI architechture, Algorithms, Frameworks and Regression data models driving several of our applications.

    Personal AI companion

    Hibot.io is a personal AI recommendation system driven by Aryabhata-I built on a familiar social network platform operating across all device forms. This tool would help humans make better decisions and help simulate scenario's based on feeds from our recommendation systems.

    Next Gen WorkSearch Platform
